## What is Aqua-Hot?

Aqua-Hot (owned by Airxcel, Inc.) manufactures hydronic heating systems for RVs — diesel, gas, and propane models. Found in high-end coaches. General NRVTA training does NOT cover hydronic heating — requires manufacturer-specific certification.

**HQ:** 7755 Miller Drive, Frederick, CO 80504
**Phone:** (303) 651-5500
**Website:** aquahot.com

### Product Lines
- **Diesel:** 125D, 250D, 400D, 450D, 600D, 675D
- **Gasoline:** 125G
- **Propane:** 125M (modular), 175 (modular), 250P, 400P
- **Specialty:** Edge Tankless Water Heater, Floor Heating Kit, Glenwood Flooring System, Wave40

## Training Program

### Course Structure
4 modules, ~8 hours total. Each module ends with an exam.

| Module | Topic | Duration |
|--------|-------|----------|
| 1 | Product & Operation (components, system operation, installation best practices, basic troubleshooting) | 3.5 hrs |
| 2 | Burner Training (diesel, gas, propane burner systems) | 2.5 hrs |
| 3 | Annual Maintenance | 1.5 hrs |
| 4 | Certification Benefits | 0.5 hrs |

### Certification Earned
- **Certified Aqua-Hot Technician**
- RVTI (RV Technical Institute) hours credited
- Join Aqua-Hot's network of certified technicians

### Format
- **Online** modules available (Module 1 offered as 4-hour online session)
- **In-person** sessions at industry events (e.g., Nov 9-13, 2026 at Paris Las Vegas)
- Registration: campaigns.airxcel.com/product-training-registration

### Eligibility
"Product Training is only open to authorized RV dealers, service centers, and technicians."

Independent mobile techs NOT explicitly included. Options to investigate:
1. Register as an Aqua-Hot Authorized Service Center first
2. Register with NRVTA certification as credential
3. Call Airxcel training directly to ask: (303) 651-5500

### Cost
Not listed publicly. Confirm during registration or phone call.
