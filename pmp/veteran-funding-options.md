## Detailed Program Breakdown

### 1. GI Bill - Licensing & Certification Test Reimbursement

**What it covers:**
- PMP exam fee reimbursement up to **$2,000 per test**
- Reimbursement only (you pay first, VA pays you back)

**What it does NOT cover:**
- PMI membership fees
- Study materials or training courses
- "Fees or costs related to getting the actual license or certification document"

**Eligibility:**
- Must be enrolled in one of: Post-9/11 GI Bill (Ch. 33), Montgomery GI Bill Active Duty (Ch. 30), Montgomery GI Bill Selected Reserve (Ch. 1606), or Survivors'/Dependents' Educational Assistance (Ch. 35)
- PMP must be on VA's approved test list (verify via VA comparison tool)

**How to apply:**
1. Take and pay for the PMP exam
2. Submit VA Form 22-0803 with receipts and test results
3. Submit via QuickSubmit or mail to regional processing office

**Source:** [VA Licensing and Certification Tests](https://www.va.gov/education/about-gi-bill-benefits/how-to-use-benefits/licensing-and-certification-tests/)

---

### 2. VA Vocational Rehabilitation & Employment (VR&E / Chapter 31)

**What it covers:**
- **100% of tuition, books, and exam fees** when part of an approved rehabilitation plan
- PMP training courses through approved providers
- PMP exam fee
- Potentially PMI membership if bundled with training
- Study materials

**What it does NOT cover:**
- Must be part of your approved employment plan (can't just request exam fee alone)

**Eligibility:**
- Veteran with a **service-connected disability rating** (typically 10%+)
- Must have an **employment barrier** (the disability affects your ability to get/keep a job)
- Apply through VA.gov or call 1-800-827-1000

**How to apply:**
1. Apply for VR&E through VA.gov
2. Get assigned a VR&E counselor
3. Develop an employment plan that includes PMP certification
4. Counselor approves training provider and exam
5. VA pays provider directly or reimburses via VBA-22-10272 form

**Key note:** This is the most comprehensive option but requires a service-connected disability. If you qualify, it can cover everything (training + exam + materials).

**Source:** [VA VR&E Home](https://www.benefits.va.gov/vocrehab/) | [PM-ProLearn VR&E Info](https://www.pm-prolearn.com/funding-options/vocrehab)

---

### 3. Black Diamond Charities (FREE - Scholarship-Based)

**What it covers:**
- **Free 3-day project management fundamentals course** (23 hours, three consecutive Saturdays via Zoom)
- After completing the course, graduates can **compete for certification scholarships** that cover:
  - PMP/CAPM exam fees
  - Study books
  - PMI membership dues
  - Ongoing certification support

**What it does NOT guarantee:**
- Scholarships are competitive (not everyone gets one)
- Must complete the fundamentals course first

**Eligibility:**
- US military veterans (honorably discharged, even years ago)
- Active duty, Reserve, National Guard
- Up to 5 military spouses per cohort
- No prerequisites or pre-study required

**How to apply:**
- Apply at [blackdiamondcharities.org/application](https://www.blackdiamondcharities.org/application)
- Sessions run in March and October (Fall 2026: Oct 3, 10, 17)
- Classes: 9am-5pm Central Time via Zoom

**Key note:** This is potentially the best fully-free option if you win a scholarship. Even without the scholarship, the free fundamentals course provides a strong foundation.

**Source:** [Black Diamond Charities](https://www.blackdiamondcharities.org/)

---

### 4. Onward to Opportunity (O2O) - Syracuse University IVMF

**What it covers:**
- **Free PMP certification training** (Business Management track)
- 5-day virtual bootcamp + 6 months of online coursework
- PMI application assistance (1-day class on applying to PMI)
- **One certification exam fee** covered upon completion of coursework and practice exam requirements

**What it does NOT cover (confirmed):**
- PMI membership (not confirmed as covered)

**Eligibility:**
- Active duty military (transitioning within 6 months, honorable discharge)
- Veterans (180+ days active duty, honorable discharge)
- National Guard and Reserve members
- Spouses of eligible service members
- Veterans must be **currently unemployed** to qualify

**How to apply:**
1. Apply at [IVMF O2O Application](https://ivmf.syracuse.edu/programs/career-training/o2o-admission/onward-to-opportunity-application/)
2. Enrollment team will contact you
3. Complete 4 days of in-person career development
4. 6 months of online coursework with milestones

**Key note:** Strong program that covers training AND one exam attempt. The unemployment requirement for veterans is important to note.

**Source:** [IVMF Career Training](https://ivmf.syracuse.edu/programs/career-training/) | [About O2O](https://ivmf.syracuse.edu/programs/career-training/about-o2o/)

### 5. ACT Now Education

**What it covers:**
- **Free PMP Exam Prep Course** (35 contact hours, meets PMI's education requirement)
- 4-week virtual program (live via Zoom)
- Covers all 3 PMP exam domains (People, Process, Business Environment)
- 90-question practice test + exam strategy guidance

**What it does NOT cover:**
- PMP exam fees (must pay separately)
- PMI membership (must pay separately)
- Textbooks (must purchase PMBOK Guide 7th Edition independently)

**Eligibility:**
- Active duty (20+ years or within 2 years of retirement)
- Veterans
- Military spouses
- Reservists and National Guard
- ACT Now volunteers

**How to apply:**
1. Attend a mandatory Saturday orientation ("Weekly Rundown") to receive enrollment code
2. Submit formal application for verification

**Key note:** Great for the 35-hour education requirement but you still need to fund the exam and membership separately. Pair with GI Bill for exam reimbursement.

**Source:** [ACT Now Education PMP Exam Prep](https://actnoweducation.org/pmp-exam)

### 6. MyCAA (Military Spouse Career Advancement Account)

**What it covers:**
- Up to **$4,000 total** (max $2,000 per fiscal year)
- Can cover PMP training courses at approved institutions
- Can cover certification exam fees
- May cover PMI membership if bundled with an approved program

**What it does NOT cover:**
- Only for spouses, not veterans themselves

**Eligibility:**
- Military **spouses only** (not veterans)
- Spouse of service member in pay grades E-1 through E-9, W-1 through W-3, O-1 through O-3
- Must have completed high school
- Sponsor must be on Title 10 military orders

**How to apply:**
- Apply at [MyCAA](https://mycaa.militaryonesource.mil/mycaa/)
- Must use approved training providers

**Key note:** This is spouse-only. Not available to veterans directly, but relevant if your spouse is active duty military in qualifying pay grades.

**Source:** [MyCAA](https://mycaa.militaryonesource.mil/mycaa/) | [Military.com MyCAA Guide](https://www.military.com/education/money-for-school/military-spouse-career-advancement-accounts-financial-aid.html)

### 7. Active Duty Military Programs (Army CA / AF COOL)

**What it covers:**
- **Army Credentialing Assistance (CA):** Up to $4,000/year for certifications including PMP
- **Air Force/Space Force COOL:** Up to $4,500 over career for certification exam fees
- Can cover exam fees and potentially PMI membership if bundled

**Eligibility:**
- **Active duty only** (not veterans)
- Army CA: Active duty Army
- AF COOL: Active duty Air Force/Space Force

**Source:** [PMI Military Resources](https://www.pmi.org/military/resources)

---

### 8. IBM SkillsBuild (VA Program)

**What it covers:**
- 1,000+ free online courses
- Focus areas: data analytics, cybersecurity, AI, cloud computing, IT support
- **Does NOT appear to offer PMP-specific training or certification support**

**Eligibility:**
- Veterans, active duty, Guard/Reserve, spouses, children, caregivers
- Must be 18+

**Relevance to PMP:** Low. This program focuses on tech skills, not project management certification.

**Source:** [VA IBM SkillsBuild](https://www.va.gov/education/other-va-education-benefits/ibm-skillsbuild-program/)

---

### 9. Vets2PM

**What it covers:**
- 10-week full-time training program
- Can earn PMP or CAPM, PMI-ACP, and Scrum Master certifications
- **Not free** - costs ~$1,287 for PMP training course
- Accepts military funding: MyCAA, AF COOL, Army CA, GI Bill, Government Purchase Card, Workforce Solutions

**Eligibility:**
- Veterans, active duty, spouses
- DoD SkillBridge eligible (active duty transitioning)

**Key note:** Good training provider that accepts multiple military funding sources, but not a free program on its own. Use as the training provider, fund it through GI Bill, VR&E, MyCAA, etc.

**Source:** [Vets2PM](https://vets2pm.com/)

---

### 10. PMI Direct (No Military-Specific Discount)

**What PMI offers everyone:**
- Free KICKOFF orientation program
- Free eLearning courses (AI, agile, PMP prep)
- Members get 30% off certifications, renewals, study resources, and eLearning
- 60+ chapters with military outreach focus (networking, mentorship)

**What PMI does NOT offer:**
- **No military/veteran discount on PMI membership** ($39/year standard)
- **No military/veteran discount on PMP exam fee** ($405 member / $555 non-member)

**Key note:** Becoming a PMI member ($39) saves you $150 on the exam ($405 vs $555), so membership pays for itself. Some local PMI chapters offer free first-year chapter membership to military (e.g., PMI Southern Maryland).

**Source:** [PMI Military Resources](https://www.pmi.org/military/resources) | [PMI Certifications](https://www.pmi.org/military/pmi-certifications-military)

## Recommended Strategy

### Best combination for a veteran (not active duty):

1. **Training (free):** Black Diamond Charities fundamentals + ACT Now Education 35-hour exam prep
2. **PMI Membership ($39):** Pay out of pocket (saves $150 on exam)
3. **Exam fee ($405 member price):** GI Bill reimbursement (up to $2,000)
4. **Backup/alternative:** Apply to O2O if currently unemployed (covers training + 1 exam)

### If you have a service-connected disability:
- **VR&E (Chapter 31)** can cover everything: training, materials, exam, potentially membership

### If your spouse is active duty military:
- **MyCAA** provides up to $4,000 that can cover training + exam

### Total out-of-pocket cost (best case): $0-$39
- Free training via Black Diamond or ACT Now
- Exam reimbursed via GI Bill
- Only PMI membership ($39) may need to come out of pocket
