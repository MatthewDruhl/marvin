# Druhl Consulting LLC - Expense Categories

IRS mileage rate: $0.725/mile (2026)

## Categories

| Category | What to Track | Examples |
|----------|--------------|---------|
| Mileage | Date, destination, business purpose, round-trip miles | Kinze on-site meetings, client meetings |
| Software & Subscriptions | Monthly/annual licenses | Claude Code, IDE licenses, GitHub, domain registration |
| Cloud Services | Hosting, compute, APIs | GCP (Cloud Run, Scheduler), Railway, AWS |
| Internet & Phone | Business-use percentage | Cell bill, internet bill |
| Home Office | Simplified: $5/sq ft (max 300 sq ft = $1,500) | RV workspace |
| Professional Development | Courses, exams, study materials | PMP exam fee, Coursera, certifications |
| Business Formation | Filing fees, legal setup | TX Form 205, registered agent, EIN |
| Business Insurance | Premiums | General liability, E&O |
| Office Supplies & Equipment | Hardware, peripherals (Section 179 eligible) | Computer, monitors, keyboard |
| Banking & Payments | Account fees, processing fees | GreenState business checking |
| Professional Services | Accountant, legal | CPA, tax prep, legal counsel |
| Marketing | Business presence | Business cards, website hosting |
| Meals & Entertainment | 50% deductible, must be business-related | Client meals during meetings |
| Travel | Lodging, parking, tolls | Overnight stays for client meetings |

## Record-Keeping Requirements

Per IRS rules, keep:
- Receipts for all expenses over $75
- Mileage log with date, destination, purpose, miles
- Bank/credit card statements
- Invoices sent and received
