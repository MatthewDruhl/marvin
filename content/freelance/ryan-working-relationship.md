### Ownership & Equity

Standard splits based on contribution:

| Contribution | Typical Equity |
|-------------|---------------|
| Idea only | 0-10% |
| Idea + domain expertise + customers | 20-40% |
| Idea + capital (funding development) | 30-50% |

Key question: What does each person bring?

### Negotiation Strategy

**Don't lead with a number.** Ask questions first to understand what Ryan is bringing:

- What's your vision for this product?
- Who are the target customers?
- What's your role after it's built?
- Are you funding development, or are we both putting in sweat equity?

**His answers determine the split:**
- If he's actively selling, managing customers, and running the business side long-term: **50/50 is fair**
- If he has an idea and needs you to build it, maintain it, and support it: **70/30 your way**, or just charge the engineering rate and he owns it outright

**Don't anchor to 50/50 before knowing what you're getting.** You're the one building and maintaining the product. That leverage matters. Let Ryan's answers guide the conversation to the right number.

### Recommendation

- Keep contractor work and product work on separate invoices, separate repos, separate agreements
- Get a one-time consultation with a business attorney before writing product code
- Start with a written agreement, even if informal, before any code is written
