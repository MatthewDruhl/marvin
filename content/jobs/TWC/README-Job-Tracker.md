## How to Use

### Adding New Applications

1. Open `job-application-tracker.csv` in Excel
2. Add new row with:
   - Date of Activity
   - Week Starting (Monday of that week)
   - Work Search Activity (Applied for job, Interview, Job Fair, etc.)
   - Company Name
   - Position Title
   - All contact information
   - Current status

### Weekly TWC Reporting

1. Open `weekly-work-search-template.csv`
2. Find the current week's row
3. Add your 4 job search activities from `job-application-tracker.csv`
4. Transfer this data to your TWC Work Search Activity Log PDF
5. Submit to TWC if requested

### Work Search Activities That Count

- Applied for job
- Submitted resume
- Attended job fair
- Interviewed (phone, video, in-person)
- Used Workforce Center
- Searched online job boards
- Networking events
- Career counseling sessions

## Tips

- **Update immediately** after each job search activity
- **Set follow-up reminders** for applications
- **Track 4+ activities per week** to stay compliant
- **Keep detailed notes** for future reference
- **Save email confirmations** as proof of application

## Required for TWC

You must complete **4 work search activities per week** to maintain unemployment benefits.

After 8 weeks of unemployment, you should:
- Reduce salary requirements
- Expand job search to more openings
- Consider adjacent job types
